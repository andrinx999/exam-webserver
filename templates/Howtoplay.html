How to play

It is very simple. You have to defeat the green enemies.

You have to find the key on the map to win the game.

You hit enemies with...and you walk with the arrow buttons.

The closer you are to the key the more enemies appear.

When you die you have to start all over again.

Be careful not to get lost on the map.
